Are you struggling to get the most out of your agile team? Do you have a brand new crew who are just getting up to speed? Or do you simply want to learn more about BDD?

After training hundreds of developers and product owners at the BBC in Behaviour Driven Development principles, and seeing huge success, for the first time Matt Wynne (author of The Cucumber Book) and Chris Parsons (well regarded speaker and trainer) are opening up their Ruby and Cucumber training courses to the wider public.

Come and equip your team to really succeed with BDD, without just paying lip-service to the ideas. We'll take developers and product owners through several days of intense hands-on workshops, and teach them to avoid the misunderstanding and gotchas teams so often fall into.

Course schedule

A ruby course specifically designed for programmers from other languages. If you've not done much Ruby before, then this day is for you. we'll get you up to speed fast on the basics that you'll need to know to comfortably use Cucumber effectively.

About Chris and Matt

Matt Wynne is one of the world's leading BDD practitioners. A programmer, coach, trainer and popular international speaker, he's the author of The Cucumber Book and a member of the Cucumber core team.

Chris Parsons is an itinerant agilist, programmer, writer and trainer. He consults in technical architecture and agile management, and trains and speaks at conferences in the USA and Europe on Ruby, Cucumber, being agile, code quality and programmer education. To keep his hand in, he also works on high profile web applications such as this one.
