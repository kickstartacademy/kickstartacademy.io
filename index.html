About Chris and Matt

Matt Wynne is one of the world's leading BDD practitioners. A programmer, coach, trainer and popular international speaker, he's the author of The Cucumber Book and a member of the Cucumber core team.

Chris Parsons is an itinerant agilist, programmer, writer and trainer. He consults in technical architecture and agile management, works on high profile web applications such as this one to keep his hand in, and trains and speaks at conferences in the USA and Europe on Ruby, Cucumber, code quality and programmer education.
